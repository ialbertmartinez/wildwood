The Story of Magnamisele WW - SOLD

ZT Magnanimus x Gisele WW) x Gazal Al Shaqab

 

Sold to to Falah Hussain Alyami of Kuwait

 

Magnamisele is an exceptional show and breeding prospect, she has all the attributes we look for in our young mares, a beautiful short dished face with huge eyes, incredibly smooth balanced body, and floating trot with great tail carriage.

Her pedigree speaks for itself; she is by the great ZT Magnanimus and out of a Gazal Al Shaqab daughter who is out of the Padrons Psyche daughter GA Psypress who is a grand daughter of the Aristocrat mare Hal Ane Versare. Her ever so important tail female line has always produced extreme beauty. Her Dam Gisele WW had a 2016 filly by Bahaa AA that is also exquisite in every way. Gisele WW is a proven producer and her daughter Magnamisele WW will be a beautiful in the show ring and a breeders dream.

Magnamisele was Top Ten yearling at the 2015 Breeders Finals in Scottsdale her first and only time shown.
