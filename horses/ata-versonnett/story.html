The Story of ATA Versonnett - SOLD

(Versace x ZA Echos Song)

 

After Versace passed away in 2008 I really wished that I had one of his daughters in my broodmare band. Somehow I was lucky enough to find that there was one near by with the help of Jimmy Kelly found that Dan Howell was looking to sell his last daughter out of ZA Echos Song . How great was that and there was an extra bonus she had a filly by her side by Major Jamaal. Her filly was adorable and she became ATA Amanda and she is a real beauty. We love this mare. She is so sweet and just wants to be loved on and she is a wonderful mom. Thank you Dan and Rosemary Howell for allowing us to own this wonderful mare and filly.

 

In foal to MF Aryan EL Thessa for Jan, 2011.
